TC Poker Analysis
A free, private analysis tool for Torn City poker. It reads your hand history and breaks down how you play, where you leak chips, and what to work on. Everything runs in your browser. No accounts, no servers, no data leaves your device.
How it works
1
Install Tampermonkey
You need the Tampermonkey browser extension first. It's free and available for Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. This is what runs the tracking script.
2
Install the TC Poker Export script
Install it from Greasy Fork. Once installed, a small tracker button appears next to the Poker header on Torn's poker page.
3
Play poker on Torn
Play as normal. The script silently captures every hand in the background and stores it in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent anywhere.
4
Export your session
Click the TC Poker Tracker button on the poker page, then hit Analyse Session. Your hand data is copied to your clipboard.
5
Paste and analyse
Come here and click Paste from Clipboard. Your full session is broken down instantly.
What it analyses
Hand types Win rates for pairs, broadway, suited connectors, ace-rag, and more
Position VPIP, fold rate, win rate, and P&L from every seat at the table
Street action How you play on each street: fold rates, aggression, bet sizing
Bet sizing Average bets by street with BB normalization across different stakes
Range grid Full 13x13 hand matrix showing win rate and frequency for every combo
Opponents Track every player you face, your record against them, and a watch list
Tables Compare your stats across different stakes and table types
Trends Session-over-session charts for win rate, VPIP, aggression, and P&L
Hand replays Click any hand to see the full action sequence with coaching notes
Automated insights The tool flags leaks, strengths, and patterns you might not spot yourself
Same device, same browser. Hand data is stored locally in the browser you play on. If you switch devices or browsers, your data won't carry over.
Nothing is stored externally. No accounts, no servers, no data collection. Everything lives in your browser.
Minimum hands. The tool works with any number, but aim for 100+ for reliable patterns. Under 50, stats will be noisy.
